Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FloatCart Pro ("we", "our", or "the App"), operated by REPLACE-WITH-COMPANY-NAME ("Company"), collects, uses, and shares information when you install or use our application through the Shopify platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you install and use FloatCart Pro, we collect the following information:

  • Store information: Your Shopify store name, store URL, store owner email address, and Shopify plan details. This is provided by Shopify during the app installation process.
  • Session data: Authentication tokens and session identifiers required to keep you logged in and to communicate securely with Shopify on your behalf.
  • Cart interaction data: Anonymous, aggregated data about how storefront visitors interact with the floating cart button, such as click counts. No personally identifiable information about your customers is collected.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information from your customers (names, emails, addresses, payment details).
  • We do not collect browsing history or tracking cookies from your storefront visitors.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third-party advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the FloatCart Pro application.
  • Authenticate your store and manage your active session.
  • Process subscription billing through the Shopify Billing API.
  • Respond to support requests you submit to us.
  • Improve the functionality and performance of the App.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your store session data is stored in a secure MongoDB database. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL). Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only. We do not store your Shopify API credentials on any publicly accessible system.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services:

  • Shopify: For app installation, authentication, billing, and storefront integration. Shopify's privacy policy applies to data processed by Shopify.
  • MongoDB Atlas: For secure session and application data storage.

We do not share your data with any other third-party services, analytics providers, or advertising networks.

6. Data Retention

We retain your store session data for as long as the App is installed on your store. When you uninstall the App, your session data is automatically deleted within 30 days. Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for internal reporting purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we store about your store.
  • Request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
  • Uninstall the App at any time from your Shopify admin, which will stop all data collection.

8. GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We process data only as necessary to provide our service. We handle all Shopify-mandated GDPR webhooks including customer data requests, customer data erasure requests, and shop data erasure requests.

9. CCPA Compliance

We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information. California residents may contact us to exercise their rights under the CCPA.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify merchants of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
